# Quzyn Cook Food Safety Standards & Agreement

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective:** Upon registration
**Entity:** Quzyn / Spheronomics, Inc. · Lewisville, TX

---

By completing registration as a Quzyn Cook, you ("Cook") agree to the following standards. These standards represent Quzyn's baseline requirements, which in many cases exceed applicable state cottage food law minimums.

---

## 1. Food Handler Certification

You must hold a valid Food Handler Certificate issued by an ANAB-accredited provider (ServSafe, StateFoodSafety, eFoodHandlers, or equivalent accepted by Quzyn). You agree to:

- Upload your current certificate during registration
- Renew your certificate before expiration and upload the renewed certificate
- Understand that selling on Quzyn with an expired certificate may result in account suspension

---

## 2. Cottage Food Law Compliance

You are responsible for understanding and complying with all applicable cottage food, home cook, and food safety laws in your state and local jurisdiction. Quzyn provides a reference guide as a convenience — it is not legal advice. You agree to:

- Review your state's current cottage food requirements before your first sale
- Obtain any state- or county-required registrations, permits, or licenses (e.g., California Class A/B registration, New York Home Processor License)
- Maintain compliance as laws change — it is your responsibility to stay current

---

## 3. Allergen Disclosure

You agree to accurately and completely disclose all allergens present in every dish you list on Quzyn. The eight major allergens (milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, and soy) must be identified for each menu item.

Failure to disclose allergens that result in a customer allergic reaction may result in immediate and permanent removal from the platform and potential legal liability.

---

## 4. Food Safety Practices

You agree to maintain the following standards in your kitchen and food preparation:

- Wash hands thoroughly before and during food preparation
- Maintain proper food storage temperatures (refrigerated items below 40°F, hot items above 140°F)
- Do not prepare or sell food when you are ill with a communicable illness (norovirus, hepatitis A, salmonella, etc.)
- Use clean, sanitized equipment and surfaces
- Source ingredients from reputable, licensed suppliers
- Never sell expired, adulterated, or misrepresented food products

---

## 5. Packaging Standards

All orders must be packaged in Quzyn-branded containers. Each container must include:

- Your name as registered on Quzyn
- Your city and state
- Dish name and full ingredients list
- All applicable allergens
- Net weight (where applicable)
- The statement: **"Made in a home kitchen not inspected by the state or local health department"** (or your state's required equivalent)
- Quzyn QR code (pre-printed on Quzyn-branded containers)

---

## 6. Kitchen Standards

You agree that your food preparation area is:

- A residential kitchen or home workspace (not a commercial kitchen — that's the point)
- Clean, organized, and free of pest infestation
- Not used simultaneously for preparing food for other commercial purposes in a way that creates contamination risk
- Available for remote photo review if requested by Quzyn for Silver tier verification

---

## 7. Platform Conduct

You agree to:

- Represent your dishes accurately — ingredients, portion sizes, and photos must reflect the actual product
- Fulfill orders you accept — consistent non-fulfillment may result in account suspension
- Respond to customer messages within a reasonable timeframe
- Accept Quzyn's rating and review system as a legitimate and transparent feedback mechanism

---

## 8. Independent Contractor Status

You are an independent contractor, not an employee of Quzyn or Spheronomics, Inc. You are responsible for:

- All federal, state, and local taxes on income earned through Quzyn (Quzyn will issue a 1099-K for earnings over $600/year)
- Your own general liability and cottage food insurance (strongly recommended — some states offer low-cost cottage food insurance)
- Compliance with all applicable business registration, tax, and licensing requirements in your jurisdiction

---

## 9. Violations & Enforcement

Quzyn reserves the right to:

- Suspend or terminate your account for verified food safety violations
- Remove listings that contain allergen misrepresentation
- Demote your certification tier for sustained poor ratings or compliance failures
- Cooperate with health authorities in the event of a food safety investigation

---

## 10. Changes to These Standards

Quzyn may update these standards as the platform grows and regulations evolve. You will be notified of material changes and given a reasonable period to review before they take effect.
