## Quzyn Launches Pilot in Dallas-Fort Worth Area, Turning Home Kitchens Into Verified Local Food Businesses

*New platform built on Food Freedom Act principles connects home cooks with neighbors seeking authentic, culturally diverse home-cooked meals — with professional food safety certification and free branded packaging*

**LEWISVILLE, TX — [DATE]** — Quzyn, a map-based food ordering platform connecting home chefs with local food lovers, today announced the launch of its neighborhood pilot in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. The platform allows home cooks to list, sell, and deliver authentic home-cooked meals — from Nigerian jollof rice to Texas-smoked brisket, Vietnamese pho, Italian ragù, and dozens of other cuisines — directly to neighbors within a few miles.

Unlike conventional food delivery apps, Quzyn puts verified, certified home cooks on an interactive neighborhood map, making the person who made your food as visible as the food itself.

### Built on the Food Freedom Act

Quzyn is designed around the principles of the Institute for Justice's Model Food Freedom Act, which has helped create thousands of new cottage food businesses across the United States.

"The Food Freedom Act exists because talented home cooks — often immigrants, parents, and people with limited capital — deserve the freedom to build a real business from their own kitchen," said Mansur Plumber, co-founder and CTO of Quzyn. "We built Quzyn to be the platform that makes that freedom work at scale. Every Quzyn cook gets professional verification, trust badges, and branded packaging that makes their home kitchen look and feel like a real business — because it is."

Texas is one of the most favorable states for cottage food entrepreneurship, with no kitchen inspection requirement, no gross sales cap, and direct-to-consumer internet sales allowed. Quzyn's pilot launches in Texas first, with planned expansion to Florida, Oklahoma, Wyoming, and other Food Freedom-friendly states.

### The Quzyn Verified Cook Program

Quzyn goes beyond state requirements with its own four-tier certification system:

- **🥉 Bronze** — Food Handler Certificate verified, identity confirmed, Quzyn Food Safety Agreement signed
- **🥈 Silver** — Background check completed, kitchen photos verified, 25+ orders, 4.5★ average
- **🥇 Gold** — Allergen training completed, 100+ orders, 4.8★ average
- **⭐ Elite** — Kitchen inspection approved, 250+ orders, 4.9★ average (invitation only)

Every verified badge appears on a cook's public profile, giving customers unprecedented transparency into who made their food and how their kitchen was verified.

### Free Starter Kit for Every New Chef

To lower the barrier to entry, Quzyn is providing every cook who completes registration with a free Quzyn-branded container starter kit — including 25 takeout boxes, 10 soup cups, and 10 kraft eco-boxes. Each container includes the cook's name, dish, allergen information, and a QR code linking to their Quzyn chef profile.
