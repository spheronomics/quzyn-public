# Quzyn Brand Guide

## Mission Statement

Quzyn exists to turn the Food Freedom Act into real economic opportunity — giving talented home cooks the platform, trust infrastructure, and branded tools to build a business from their own kitchen, while connecting food lovers with authentic, handmade meals from their own neighborhood.

## Voice & Tone

**We are:** Warm, community-driven, joyful, honest, inclusive, empowering
**We are not:** Corporate, formal, tech-startup-cold, exclusive, pretentious

| Situation | Tone |
|---|---|
| Marketing headlines | Warm and bold. Conversational Lora serif. |
| Chef onboarding | Encouraging and clear. No jargon. |
| Food safety / compliance | Direct and serious, but never scary. |
| Error messages | Helpful, not punishing. |
| Success moments | Celebratory — "You're in, Chef!" |

**Words we use:** neighbors, community, real, home, handmade, authentic, culture, earn, kitchen, cook, flavor

**Words we avoid:** disrupting, marketplace, gig, vendor, user, consumer, synergy

## Typography

### Display (Headlines)
- **Font:** Lora (Google Fonts)
- **Weights:** 400 (italic), 600, 700
- **Use:** All H1–H3 headlines, chef names, section titles, prices
- **Character:** Warm, editorial, trustworthy — feels like a food magazine

### Body
- **Font:** Nunito (Google Fonts)
- **Weights:** 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900
- **Use:** All body text, UI labels, buttons, captions
- **Character:** Friendly, rounded, highly legible

### Handwritten Accent
- **Font:** Caveat (Google Fonts)
- **Weights:** 500, 600, 700
- **Use:** Section eyebrow tags ("Meet your neighbors"), hero sublines, personality touches
- **Character:** Personal, warm, human

### Scale
```
Display:  clamp(2.5rem, 5vw, 4.5rem)  — hero H1
H2:       clamp(1.9rem, 3.5vw, 3rem)  — section titles
H3:       1.1–1.3rem                  — card titles
Body:     0.88–1.05rem                — paragraphs
Small:    0.75–0.82rem                — captions, hints
```

## Photography Style

- **Food photos:** Real dishes, real plates, natural light, honest — no over-styled food photography
- **Kitchen photos:** Real home kitchens — clean but lived-in, not sterile
- **Chef photos:** Candid cooking moments preferred over posed portraits
- **Containers:** Show Quzyn-branded containers with food inside — always sealed or being opened
- **Avoid:** Stock photo clichés, artificial food styling, dark moody restaurant aesthetics

---

## Container Brand Standards

Quzyn provides three container types to home chefs:
1. **White takeout box** — for rice dishes, stir-fries, pasta, general mains
2. **Kraft eco-box** — for salads, lighter dishes, eco-conscious presentation
3. **White soup cup + lid** — for soups, stews, curries, pho, chili

Every container displays:
- Quzyn logo (printed or stickered)
- Chef name + cuisine flag
- Dish name
- Allergen info
- "Made in a home kitchen" statement (required by most state cottage food laws)
- QR code linking to chef's Quzyn profile

## Certification Tier Badges

Display on chef profiles exactly as follows:

```
🥉 Bronze Cook       — entry level, Food Handler Certified
🥈 Silver Cook       — background checked, kitchen verified, 25+ orders, 4.5★+
🥇 Gold Cook         — allergen trained, 100+ orders, 4.8★+
⭐ Elite Chef        — kitchen inspected, 250+ orders, 4.9★+, invitation only
```

Trust badges displayed below tier badge:
```
🟢 Food Handler Certified
🟢 Identity Verified
🟢 Background Checked
🟢 Texas Cottage Food Compliant  (or relevant state)
🟢 Kitchen Photos Verified
🟢 Allergen Training Completed
🟢 Top Rated Cook
🟢 100 Orders Completed
```
